# DevBooks: Knife (Epic Slicing)

Slice an Epic into a convergent Slice queue, and write a machine-readable Knife Plan (mandatory for high-risk or epic changes).

## Usage

/devbooks:knife [args]

## Args

$ARGUMENTS

## Notes

- When `request_kind=epic` or `risk_level=high`, Knife Plan is mandatory before execution.
- Knife Plan location: `dev-playbooks/specs/_meta/epics/<epic_id>/knife-plan.yaml`
