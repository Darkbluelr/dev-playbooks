# /devbooks:judge

Triggers Skill: `devbooks-proposal-judge`

## Purpose

Make a ruling on the proposal stage, outputting Approved/Revise/Rejected and writing back to the Decision Log in proposal.md.

## Typical Use Cases

- Final ruling needed after proposal debate is complete
- Need to decide whether a proposal can proceed to implementation
- Recording decision rationale and conditions

Please invoke `devbooks-proposal-judge` to execute the corresponding task.
