# /devbooks:challenger

Triggers Skill: `devbooks-proposal-challenger`

## Purpose

Challenge and identify gaps in proposal.md, pointing out risks, omissions, and inconsistencies with conclusions. Discover missing acceptance criteria and uncovered scenarios.

## Typical Use Cases

- Proposal needs challenge review after completion
- Need to identify potential risks and omissions
- Execute the Challenger role in the proposal debate workflow

Please invoke `devbooks-proposal-challenger` to execute the corresponding task.
