# /devbooks:backport

Triggers Skill: `devbooks-design-backport`

## Purpose

Backport new constraints, conflicts, and gaps discovered during implementation to design.md, keeping the design as the golden truth, with annotated decisions and impacts.

## Typical Use Cases

- Design omissions or conflicts discovered during implementation
- Need to update design documents to reflect reality
- Maintaining consistency between design and implementation

Please invoke `devbooks-design-backport` to execute the corresponding task.
